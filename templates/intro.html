Will Artificial Intelligence Take Over the World?

Will AI take over the world? From what I have concluded, yes it will, and there are many different outcomes. These outcomes could be the Disney movie Wall-E becomes nonfiction, AI creates global tension by sending fake messages to other countries, people could use AI to control other groups of people. My personal favorite one of these outcomes is Wall-E becoming historically accurate.

In BBC’s article “Artificial Intelligence could lead to extinction, experts warn,” it shows what “experts” think, however, this article speaks of the human race becoming dependent on AI and controlling our lives. This is important because there is evidence of this already beginning. One example of this is AI toasters; I say AI toasters because one exists, and it also shows how we are putting AI in about every product imaginable. One of the things a fellow human does not need is a personal AI assistant to toast your bread for you, and if humans get used to stuff like this they will not only be like the people from the Wall-E movie but also be in the same shape of a person from Wall-E, which is not a good thing. The bigger problem would be if there were to be a time where an AI-dependent human loses access to this tool, they will have no idea what to do, and go mad. It would be like how most people who like to consume content on their smartphones would go bananas if they lost their phone, but they wouldn’t if they had never had one to begin with.

The scariest outcome is AI getting involved in war activities. The thing about AI is that it does not have feelings, fears, or the ability to feel pain, meaning that it will go through whatever it takes to complete its objective, even if they are destroyed in the process. The only loss of using AI for warfare is financial, so there isn’t that much to lose compared to current soldiers. AI would make little to no mistakes on the battlefield and do whatever it’s told with no guilt or mercy, making it very dangerous to any of its human targets.

One of the most likely outcomes is people taking away other people’s access to AI tools, and using it for controlling everyone below them. If one were to restrict access to such a tool, they could create monopolies, use it for warfare, or technologically upgrade faster than every other country or group, becoming supreme to everyone else.

For now, however, this is not a topic to worry about, but it is in my opinion one to think about. By the time AI develops enough to take everyone’s jobs away, we might not even exist anymore. One can only hope that what awaits in the future is positive, so it is always better to be safe than sorry.
